WINTER SCHOOL OF AI
AND ROBOTICS

Co-sponsored by Centre for Excellence in Artificial Intelligence, IIT Kharagpur

Objectives

The Winter School of AI and Robotics is a groundbreaking venture by Technology Robotix Society in association with Centre for Artificial Intelligence, IIT Kharagpur and sponsored by IEEE. As a school focused on real-life applicatons, aided by a plethora of problem statements, it develops the firsthand skills of sudents and prepares them for industrial usage of advanced concepts in three core verticals.

OVERVIEW

The Winter School spans across the first half of December and will be conducted in two phases, both of a week long duration. All three verticals will be taught in both phases. Students who have the mandatory EAA camps in the first week of December can attend the second phase of the workshop. Interested students can attend both phases. Students can only study one vertical in one phase.

The School will be divided into 3 core verticals namely Computer Vision, Autonomous Robotics and IoT, and Machine Learning. While Computer Vision and Autonomous Robotics are for both first and second years, Machine Learning is exclusively for second years. Experts on these verticals from around the world will also be giving guest lectures on hot topics in today's industry!
Refer to the syllabus for more details.
